# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Social Media Auto-Poster ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our desktop application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **API Credentials**: OAuth tokens and credentials for Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram
- **Video Content**: Videos you choose to upload through the App
- **Metadata**: Titles, descriptions, and tags you provide for your videos

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Application Logs**: Error logs and debugging information stored locally
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the App (stored locally only)

## 3. How We Store Your Information

### 3.1 Local Storage
- All credentials are stored **locally on your device only**
- Credentials are **encrypted** using industry-standard encryption
- We do **NOT** transmit your credentials to any server we control
- We do **NOT** have access to your API credentials or tokens

### 3.2 Data Location
- All data is stored in your local application data folder
- No data is sent to external servers except:
  - Direct API calls to social media platforms (Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram)
  - These calls are made directly from your device to the platforms

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Authenticate with social media platforms on your behalf
- Upload videos to the platforms you select
- Provide the core functionality of the App

## 5. Information Sharing

### 5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Information
- We do **NOT** sell your information
- We do **NOT** share your information with third parties
- We do **NOT** have access to your information

### 5.2 Direct Platform Communication
- The App communicates directly with social media platforms using their official APIs
- Your content and credentials are sent directly to the platforms you choose
- These transmissions are subject to each platform's privacy policy

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:
- **Facebook/Instagram** (Meta Platforms, Inc.)
- **TikTok** (ByteDance Ltd.)
- **YouTube** (Google LLC)

Each platform has its own privacy policy:
- Facebook/Instagram: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/
- TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
- YouTube: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 7. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- **Encryption**: All credentials are encrypted at rest
- **Local Storage**: No cloud storage of your credentials
- **Secure Transmission**: HTTPS for all API communications
- **No Logging**: We do not log your credentials or tokens

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your stored credentials (locally on your device)
- **Delete**: Remove your credentials at any time through the App
- **Disconnect**: Revoke access to any platform at any time
- **Export**: Your data is stored locally and accessible to you

## 9. Data Retention

- Credentials are stored until you disconnect a platform or uninstall the App
- You can delete all stored data by disconnecting all platforms
- Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 12. Open Source

This application is open source. You can review the code to verify our privacy practices at our GitHub repository.

## 13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.

## 14. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

---

**Summary**: We take your privacy seriously. Your credentials are encrypted and stored only on your device. We never have access to your information, and we never share it with anyone. The App only communicates directly with the social media platforms you choose to use.
